About Me

I am a creative coder who enjoys exploring and visualizing complex concepts using the p5.js library. My projects cover a range of themes including Perlin noise, cellular automata, and much more. I believe in the power of visual representations to make intricate ideas accessible to a broader audience.

On this website, you can explore some of my interactive sketches and learn more about the ideas behind them.
